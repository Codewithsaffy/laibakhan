Types of Mountains

Mountains can be classified into different types.

1- Fold Mountains

Formed by the collision of tectonic plates that cause the Earth's crust to fold. These are the most common type of mountain.

2- Volcanic Mountains

Formed by volcanic activity when molten rock (magma) from beneath the Earth's surface erupts, cools, and piles up.

3- Dome Mountains

Created when magma pushes the Earth's crust upward, but does not break through the surface. The uplifted rock forms a dome shape.

4- Plateau Mountains

Formed by erosion rather than tectonic activity. Plateaus are raised flat areas that are eroded over time into mountain-like formations.

5- Fault-Block Mountains

Created when large areas of Earth's crust break and move along faults, leading to blocks of crust being uplifted or tilted.
